Roll a Ball Project - Unity3D version 5

-May be tested by navigating to "Roll a ball\Build\Roll a Ball"
	
	+ WASD keys are used to move the ball

	+ You do not need Unity3D's editor to use this project!

-Simple Unity3D project made using 3D materials and physics to keep the ball moving

-The goal is for the player to collect all the pickups with a ball
	
	+The following items are controlled or altered by C# properties

		~Control of camera

		~Controls of player's ball

		~Rotation of the ball every frame

		~Speed of player's ball

		~Rotation of the pickups

		~The object the camera focuses on
